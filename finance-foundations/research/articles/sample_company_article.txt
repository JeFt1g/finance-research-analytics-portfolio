Apple Sample reported revenue growth of 7% and net income growth of 10% in the latest fiscal year. Gross margin improved to 46%, while operating margin remained strong despite higher research and development spending. Management said services revenue and installed base growth helped stabilize earnings.

The company returned capital through buybacks, which can improve earnings per share and return on equity. Analysts still noted risks from regulation, supply chain concentration, foreign exchange volatility, and competition in premium smartphones. The balance sheet remains liquid, but investors should monitor leverage, working capital, and whether growth assumptions justify the current valuation multiple.
