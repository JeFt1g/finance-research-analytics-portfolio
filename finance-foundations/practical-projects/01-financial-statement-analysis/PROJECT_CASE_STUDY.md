# Financial Statement Analysis Case Study

## Problem

Analyze whether an Apple-style sample company shows improving revenue, profitability, liquidity, and leverage.

## Data Used

`data/apple_sample_financials.csv` contains fake annual financial statement data from 2020 through 2025.

## Methods

- Revenue and net income trend analysis
- Gross, operating, and net margin calculation
- ROE, current ratio, debt-to-equity, and asset turnover
- Markdown report generation
- Matplotlib chart export

## Key Calculations

Margins are calculated as profit divided by revenue. ROE is net income divided by shareholders' equity. Current ratio is current assets divided by current liabilities. Debt-to-equity is total debt divided by shareholders' equity.

## Example Insights

The sample company shows revenue growth, strong ROE, improving margins, and lower leverage, while liquidity remains close enough to 1.0 to deserve monitoring.
