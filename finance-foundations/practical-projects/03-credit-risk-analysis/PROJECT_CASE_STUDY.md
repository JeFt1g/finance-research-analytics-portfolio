# Credit Risk Analysis Case Study

## Problem

Assess borrower risk using a transparent sample credit score, default probability estimate, and risk segment.

## Credit Scoring Method

The model adjusts a starting score for debt-to-income, missed payments, existing debt, employment status, income, and loan size.

## Default Risk Method

Default probability is estimated with a rule-based logistic-style transformation of the score plus risk-driver penalties.

## Risk Segments

Borrowers are assigned to low, medium, high, or very high risk categories.

## Example Borrower Insights

The report highlights safest and riskiest sample borrowers and shows how high debt-to-income and missed payments increase estimated risk.

## Limitations

The model is fake and not validated. Real lending work requires fairness testing, compliance review, calibration, and monitoring.

## Real-World Use

This project demonstrates credit analytics structure, explainable features, risk segmentation, and portfolio monitoring.
