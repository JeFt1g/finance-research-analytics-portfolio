# Flashcards

Q: What does Black-Scholes estimate?
A: The fair value of a European call or put option under simplifying assumptions.

Q: What does implied volatility represent?
A: The volatility level that makes the model price equal the market option price.

Q: Why can Black-Scholes fail during crises?
A: Volatility, liquidity, jumps, and hedging assumptions can break down.

Q: Which Greek measures sensitivity to the underlying price?
A: Delta.
