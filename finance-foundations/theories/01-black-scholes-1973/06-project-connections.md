# Project Connections

## Option Pricing Engine

Use Black-Scholes to price calls and puts from spot price, strike price, time to expiration, volatility, and the risk-free rate.

## Volatility Analysis

Compare historical volatility with implied volatility to explain why option prices move even when the stock price does not.

## Portfolio Risk

Use Greeks to show how option positions add nonlinear exposure to a portfolio.

## Risk Dashboard

Track delta, gamma, theta, vega, implied volatility, and stress scenarios.
