# Flashcards

Q: What is the core claim of EMH?
A: Prices reflect available information quickly enough that consistent risk-adjusted outperformance is difficult.

Q: What is semi-strong-form efficiency?
A: Public information is already reflected in prices.

Q: Does EMH mean prices are always correct?
A: No. It means beating prices after costs and risk adjustment is hard.

Q: Which project tests EMH directly?
A: Market Efficiency Tester.
