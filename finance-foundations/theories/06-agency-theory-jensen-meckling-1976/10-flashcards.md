# Flashcards

Q: What is the principal-agent problem?
A: A conflict where managers may act in their own interest instead of owners' interests.

Q: What are agency costs?
A: Costs from monitoring, bonding, and residual conflicts between principals and agents.

Q: Why does executive compensation matter?
A: Incentives can align or misalign management with shareholders.

Q: Which practical project uses agency theory?
A: Corporate Governance Analyzer and Financial Statement Analysis.
