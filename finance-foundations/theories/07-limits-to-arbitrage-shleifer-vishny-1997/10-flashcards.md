# Flashcards

Q: Why can mispricing persist?
A: Arbitrageurs face funding limits, liquidity risk, short-selling constraints, and career risk.

Q: What does "being right too early" mean?
A: A trade can be fundamentally correct but lose money before convergence.

Q: Why do investor withdrawals matter?
A: They can force arbitrageurs to close positions before mispricing corrects.

Q: Which projects connect to this theory?
A: Mispricing Risk Dashboard, Market Efficiency Tester, and Portfolio Analysis.
