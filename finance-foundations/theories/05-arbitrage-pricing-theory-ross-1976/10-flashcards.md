# Flashcards

Q: How does APT differ from CAPM?
A: APT allows multiple risk factors instead of one market beta.

Q: What is factor exposure?
A: Sensitivity of an asset or portfolio to a risk factor.

Q: What does no-arbitrage mean?
A: Prices should not allow a riskless profit after accounting for constraints and costs.

Q: Which project fits APT?
A: Multifactor Model Lab.
