# Flashcards

Q: What does CAPM estimate?
A: Expected return based on risk-free rate, beta, and market risk premium.

Q: What does beta measure?
A: Sensitivity to systematic market risk.

Q: What is alpha?
A: Return above or below what CAPM predicts.

Q: How does CAPM connect to valuation?
A: It can estimate cost of equity for discount rates.
