# Flashcards

Q: What is the central idea of Modern Portfolio Theory?
A: Portfolio risk depends on variance and covariance, not just standalone asset risk.

Q: Why do correlations matter?
A: Low correlations can reduce total portfolio volatility.

Q: What is the efficient frontier?
A: The set of portfolios with the best expected return for a given risk level.

Q: Which practical app uses MPT most directly?
A: Python Portfolio Analysis.
