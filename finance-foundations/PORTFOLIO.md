## Research System

The research layer stores raw sources in `research/articles/`, processes them through `shared/article_reader/`, and saves markdown analysis to `research/processed/`. The reader extracts summaries, important numbers, companies, risk terms, theory connections, project connections, and possible app ideas.

The system is designed to connect articles to:

- Theory notes in `theories/`
- Practical projects in `practical-projects/`
- Portfolio case studies and report sections
