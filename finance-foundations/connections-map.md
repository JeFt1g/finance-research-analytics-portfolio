# Connections Map

## One-Sentence Summary

A map of how the theories connect through risk, information, incentives, and pricing.

## Why It Matters

The theories are more useful as a system than as isolated chapters.

## Core Idea

## Risk
MPT, CAPM, APT, and Black-Scholes.

## Information
Market Efficiency and Limits to Arbitrage.

## Incentives
Agency Theory.

## Pricing
Black-Scholes, CAPM, and APT.

## Where Theories Conflict
- Efficient Market Hypothesis vs Limits to Arbitrage.
- CAPM vs APT.
- MPT assumptions vs real-world behavior.

## Key Terms

- Risk
- Information
- Incentives
- Pricing
- Mispricing

Framework: identify the problem, choose the theory, then check which assumptions another theory challenges.
