# Finance Foundations Timeline

## One-Sentence Summary

A 1952 to 1997 timeline of the theories in this repo.

## Why It Matters

The sequence shows how later theories extend or challenge earlier ones.

## Core Idea

- 1952: Markowitz, Modern Portfolio Theory.
- 1960s: Sharpe and related development of CAPM.
- 1970: Fama, Market Efficiency.
- 1973: Black-Scholes option pricing.
- 1976: Ross, Arbitrage Pricing Theory.
- 1976: Jensen and Meckling, Agency Theory.
- 1997: Shleifer and Vishny, Limits to Arbitrage.

Framework: portfolio risk, market risk, information, options, factors, incentives, and arbitrage constraints.
