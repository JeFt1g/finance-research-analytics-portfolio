# Pitch Deck Outline

Use this as a short deck structure for a stock pitch, valuation project, credit recommendation, or portfolio review.

## Slide 1: Title And Recommendation

- Project title.
- Company/asset/borrower/portfolio.
- Recommendation.

## Slide 2: Situation

- What problem is being analyzed?
- Why does it matter?

## Slide 3: Data And Assumptions

- Data source.
- Key assumptions.
- Important limitations.

## Slide 4: Analysis

- Most important chart or table.
- Method used.
- Interpretation.

## Slide 5: Recommendation

- Final view.
- Upside/downside or risk category.
- Biggest risks.

## Slide 6: Appendix

- Formula notes.
- Sensitivity table.
- Extra charts.

## Recorded Walkthrough

Recommended length: 2 to 3 minutes.

Suggested structure:

1. State the project and recommendation.
2. Explain the data and method.
3. Show the key chart or table.
4. Explain the main risk.
5. Close with what the project proves about your skills.
