# Portfolio Assets

Use this folder for career-facing materials that sit next to the finance projects.

## Folders

- `resume/`: place the final resume PDF here before publishing.
- `writing-samples/`: investment memo and market commentary templates.
- `presentation/`: pitch deck outline and recorded walkthrough notes.

## Rules Before Publishing

- Remove private or confidential information.
- Use public or fake data only.
- Proofread every number and formula.
- Include educational disclaimers for investment, valuation, and credit work.
